About Me

Hello. I am an academic researcher at Tohoku University. I research natural language processing at Tohoku NLP Group (FaiLab). Currently, I work on the development of dialogue systems and the analysis of Japanese language capabilities in large language models.

Research Interests

  • Natural Language Processing
  • Machine Learning
  • Discourse
  • Dialogue System
  • Japanese

Current Questions

  • Realizing advanced, full-duplex dialogue in dialogue systems comparable to human-human conversation.
  • Investigating how well large language models understand argument ellipsis in Japanese and how that understanding affects downstream tasks.
